Cold War peak basing costs (1960s–70s) reflect Vietnam-era global posture. Post-Cold War drawdown cut overseas forces ~60%. Post-9/11 surge driven by Iraq/Afghanistan operational costs.

// What Does Basing Cost Include? S20
The "true cost" of overseas basing is contested. Direct DoD costs include:
▸ RAND estimates full lifecycle costs at 2–3× direct costs
▸ Environmental cleanup, post-closure costs often excluded
▸ Personnel entitlements (COLA, BAH) = ~40% of total cost
// Per-Troop Annual Cost by Location S20
Fully-loaded cost per stationed troop per year (salary + ops + infra + logistics)
Costs are higher in Japan/S.Korea due to: SOFA requirements, dollar/yen exchange exposure, high local infrastructure standards, and remote island logistics (Okinawa).
